About UCOB

Empowering a sustainable future through responsible recycling.

Our Mission

The Used Cooking Oil Buying & Selling Platform (UCOB) was born from a simple college project with a grand vision: to prevent environmental damage caused by improper disposal of used cooking oil, and to create an accessible marketplace where waste is transformed into valuable resources like biofuel.

We bridge the gap between households, restaurants, and industrial buyers. By providing a seamless, reliable platform, we ensure that every drop of used oil is recycled ethically, benefiting both our planet and our economy.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We prioritize the health of our planet by ensuring harmful waste products are repurposed safely.

Community

We empower individuals and local businesses to participate in the circular economy effortlessly.

Transparency

We provide a secure, clear, and trustworthy platform for all buying and selling transactions.
